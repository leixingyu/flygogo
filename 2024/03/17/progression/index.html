Development Progression & Plans

Quick Start

You know, it’s funny how inspiration strikes sometimes. I’ve always been a gamer, and I guess you could say life threw me a curveball when I ended up in a wheelchair. But instead of dwelling on the challenges, I started seeing things from a different perspective. One day, while navigating through the bustling city streets, it hit me – why not make a game that reflects my own experiences and sheds light on the obstacles faced by people with mobility impairments?

I mean, gaming has always been my escape, my way of exploring new worlds and overcoming challenges. So, I figured, why not use that same platform to raise awareness and foster empathy? It just made sense to me. Plus, I had this vision of a precision platformer, where players would step into the shoes of a wheelchair-bound protagonist and navigate through all sorts of obstacles. It felt like the perfect blend of fun and meaningfulness.

So, armed with my passion for gaming and a newfound sense of purpose, I dove headfirst into development. Drawing from my own experiences and fueled by a desire to make a positive impact, I poured my heart and soul into creating a game that would not only entertain but also educate and inspire. And you know what? It’s been one heck of a journey, but seeing the impact the game has had – sparking conversations, changing perceptions – makes it all worth it.
