Plans

First of all, what is the goal of this game, where is it heading?

  • At the very least, I will deliver this game on steam with only playable status as early access within 300 days, projected to be end of 2024;
  • If the game has even the mildest support from the community, I will continue on getting it to my standard quality bar, with finished content.
  • If I have more support, we’ll see it in a kickstarter and cover more detail from there.
